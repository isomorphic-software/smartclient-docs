## Attr: LoadingIndicatorSettings.target

### Description
Target position / sizing for the loading indiciator. May be specified as a four element array `[left,top,width,height]` or as a DOM element, in which case the indicator will be sized and positioned to match the client bounding rect of the target element. If unspecified, the loading indicator will be sized to fit its content and centered on the page.

**Flags**: IRW
